Starstream Striker

Welcome to Space War

In the endless reaches of space, your spaceship speeds ahead, facing a constant stream of dangers from all sides. Dodge asteroids and blast away enemy ships and creatures with your continuous barrage of bullets.
Your mission: Eliminate as many enemies as possible, establishing yourself as the foremost space warrior and safeguard the galaxy from annihilation.

Pilot your spaceship using arrow keys.
The spaceship fires bullets continuously, destroy enemy ships (4 bullets), space creatures (3 bullets) and asteroids (2 bullets).
Each successful blast earns you 1 point.
Don't let your spaceship collide with them and also dodge your spaceship from enemy bullets, you will have 3 lives.
Beware of power cards for strategic advantages.
